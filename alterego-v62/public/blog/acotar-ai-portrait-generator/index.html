What is the ACOTAR style direction?

The ACOTAR theme in ALTER.EGO draws from the visual language of the book series — dark Illyrian armour, sweeping capes, moonlit mountain landscapes, glowing magic, ancient forest settings and the distinctive tattoos of the Night Court. The AI has been prompted to capture the cinematic, high-fantasy aesthetic that ACOTAR readers picture when they're deep in the books.

Depending on your features and the prompt interpretation, you might come out as a warrior standing on a cliff edge above a winding canyon, a Fae court member surrounded by glowing wildflowers, or a High Lord with flames in both hands beneath a full moon. Every generation is slightly different — which makes it genuinely addictive to keep generating.

How to generate your ACOTAR portrait

The process takes about 60 seconds from upload to result:

  • Step 1 — Upload a selfie. Clear, well-lit, facing forward. The AI reads your facial features to anchor the generation to you specifically. A clean selfie in good light gives the best face lock.
  • Step 2 — Select the ACOTAR style. Scroll through the 16 style directions and tap ACOTAR. Each style has multiple visual interpretations that rotate on every generation.
  • Step 3 — Generate. The AI analyses your photo, builds a cinematic prompt, and renders your portrait in under 30 seconds.
  • Step 4 — Share. Download your portrait or share directly to Instagram. Tag us — we love seeing the results.

Tips for the best ACOTAR result

Use a front-facing photo. The AI needs to clearly read your facial features. A slight angle is fine but a direct front-facing shot gives the strongest face lock — meaning the output actually looks like you rather than a generic fantasy character.

Good lighting makes a big difference. Natural window light or any soft indoor light works well. Avoid harsh shadows across your face or very dark images.

Generate multiple times. Each generation interprets the ACOTAR world slightly differently. One might place you in the Illyrian mountains, another in an ancient forest. The first result isn't always the best — run it 2-3 times and pick your favourite.

Try the Acotar style alongside Painterly and Sovereign. If you love the ACOTAR aesthetic, the Painterly style (Old Masters portrait treatment) and Sovereign style (royal court, velvet and gold) give similarly dramatic fantasy results with a different mood.
